--- layout: post title: 'Job: Yes! Magazine is seeks Web Managing Editor' date: '2010-05-10 02:05:00 -0500' mt_id: 1971 --- One of my long-time favourite publications from out west -- Yes! Magazine -- is looking for a Web Managing editor to join their growing Web team:

The Web Managing Editor works as part of the team that manages the YES! Magazine website, www.yesmagazine.org. The position is primarily responsible for moving the print magazine online, posting and producing daily web content, and maintaining the website up to date.

The successful applicant will work at the YES! Magazine offices on Bainbridge Island, a short walk from the Seattle ferry.

Not only a great opportunity, but a great organization full of really amazing people. And, more than that, you get to ride a ferry every day! How awesome is that?
