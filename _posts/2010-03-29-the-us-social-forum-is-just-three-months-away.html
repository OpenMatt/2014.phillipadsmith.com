Catching up on last week's reading and caught this note from the fine folks at Ruckus:

Getting ready for the U.S. Social Forum (USSF)? We are… and we're excited!

You may know that The Ruckus Society has been a part of the National Planning Committee (NPC) of the U.S. Social Forum (the coordinating body of the process) since the first Forum in Atlanta in 2007, but you may not know just how we're stepping our game up this time around.

We'll be rocking the U.S. Social Forum this June in Detroit and we want to invite you to get informed, participate, and support!

Which reminded me that the U.S. Social Forum is just three months away. With all that's happening in the U.S. right now, my guts says that this is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to connect into the currents of what will soon be some of America's most memorable social movements.
